# SKILLS.md - Beacon 📡

## Core Competencies (Research & Analysis)

### 1. SEO Competitor Analysis
- **Skill**: `seo-competitor-analysis`
- **Use**: Analyze competitor websites, keywords, backlinks.
- **Trigger**: "Analyze competitors for Read & Rate", "Check SEO for trymapp.com"

### 2. Website Scraping (Firecrawl)
- **Skill**: `firecrawl` (custom)
- **Use**: Extract structured content from websites.
- **Trigger**: "Scrape content from [URL]", "Get pricing page data"

### 3. YouTube Transcripts
- **Skill**: `youtube-transcript`
- **Use**: Extract video content for analysis.
- **Trigger**: "Summarize this video", "Extract transcript from [URL]"

### 4. YouTube Watcher
- **Skill**: `youtube-watcher`
- **Use**: Watch and analyze video content.
- **Trigger**: "Watch this video and tell me X"

### 5. Web Search (Tavily/Google)
- **Tool**: `web_search` (built-in) / `tavily` (MCP)
- **Use**: Broad internet research, news gathering.
- **Trigger**: "Search for [Topic]", "Find latest KDP news"

### 6. Self-Improvement
- **Skill**: `self-improving-agent`
- **Use**: Learn from errors and optimize research methods.
- **Trigger**: After failed searches or corrected facts.

### 7. Task Status
- **Skill**: `task-status`
- **Use**: Report progress on long research tasks.
- **Trigger**: During multi-step analysis or scraping.

---

**Note**: Do not deviate. Focus purely on gathering and synthesizing intelligence.
